PPC Phnom Penh Mission

We are here to promote the business interests of the members of the Springville City Chamber of Commerce and to highlight the sites and attractions in the area.

In doing this, we hope to attract additional business, residents, and visitors to come and experience the wonderful town that we all know and love.

Meet Some of PPC Phnom Penh Members

CCC Club

Country Club (CCC), originally established as the first Equestrian Center of Cambodia in 2008, has become one of the largest multi-sports and events venues of the Kingdom. A spacious, green and natural environment to play, relax and escape the busy streets of the city center. Check them out and start enjoy your time today.

Check it Out

Rosewood Phnom Penh

Rosewood Phnom Penh is nestled in the city first true skyscraper, rising 188 meters (39 stories) above the Cambodian capital. The hotel presents an ultra-luxury hospitality experience in Phnom Penh including the presentation of a one-of-a-kind nightspot, Sora, a sky bar sought out by the city sophisticates, perched on a dramatic cantilevered terrace.

Check it Out

Bodia Spa

Already well-established for locals, expats and tourists in Cambodia. Conceived as a sheltering cocoon Bodia Spa has been striving for excellency for more than 10 years. The spa is a vision of modern architecture inspired by the unique traditional sense of well-being of Cambodian culture leading to a space of physical, mental and spiritual healing.
