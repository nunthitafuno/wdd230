Our Purpose

We are here to promote the business interests of the members of the Springville City Chamber of Commerce and to highlight the sites and attractions in the area.

In doing this, we hope to attract additional business, residents, and visitors to come and experience the wonderful town that we all know and love.

Meet Some of Our Members

Funfinity

Funfinity is a family owned and operated. They carry a variety of toys, games, books, and chocolate supplies. Everyday they do their best to help customers find the perfect gift. Their employees are committed to giving customers the best possible experience. Check them out and start having fun today.

Mountain Rock Music

Mountain Rock Music shares their passion for music with you and your family. They pride themselves for their musical knowledge, and promise customers the best service and experience. They provide guitars, band instruments, print music, rentals, repairs, drum accessories, and any other musical accessory imaginable.

Pier 49 Pizza

In 1991, Pier 49 fired up its first oven. More than two decades later, their quest and passion remains the same: Create the most mouthwateringly delicious gourmet pizzas. They’re sure you’ll agree with the critics and guests: Pier 49 is the best…anywhere! Savor our famous sourdough crust, premium mozzarella and five imported cheeses.
