Target Audience Description

The target audience of the whetherweather.com website:

Who: college student, couples, resident members, and tourist that wanna exploing in learning what upcoming events are free to take a part in the cities. live music, bikers, workshops, poetry night, resident members and tourist wanna know the current and forecasted weather with the list area given.

Age: 19 to 89

Our audience mainly uses their cellular devices to access information using smartphone, tablets or computers.

Income: Income levels can be from the young unemployed, to the $50,000 household and above.

Goals: To manage and plan their upcoming events, and make sure they be aware with the dress that they should wear to fit the weather conditions for outdoor activities.
